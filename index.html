Why Confidence Matters Early

Many young students hesitate to raise their hands, answer questions, or take part — not because they don’t know, but because they lack confidence. That gap grows unless addressed early.

Problem

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries answered to reduce friction before enrolling.

Who is this course for?

Students in Grades 3 to 5 who need help speaking up in class, participating, or gaining early academic self-confidence.

How long is the course?

It’s a 2-week structured microcourse with 4–5 short sessions per week plus practice activities.

What if my child is shy initially?

The curriculum starts with very low-pressure soft activities and gradually builds comfort. Mentors are trained to be empathetic and encouraging.

Do parents get updates?

Yes. Weekly progress summaries are shared, and there’s a short sync at the end of Week 1 and Week 2 with the mentor.
